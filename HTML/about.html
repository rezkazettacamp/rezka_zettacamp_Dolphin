ABOUT NEOS FAMOUS MUSIC

Neos Famous Music
Established in 2021 which is overseen by two young entrepreneurs from Yogyakarta who sell various kinds of musical instruments then develop by opening music courses. Starting from a house, gradually the number of enthusiasts increased and it was developed and then changed addresses. Supported by qualified facilities and mentors who are very professional in their respective fields and are very friendly, it makes the students who are studying at Neos Famous Music more comfortable and focused on training.
After the course process is complete, a Mini Concert & Grand Concert will be held, I-Best (The Best Performance), student report cards and grade increase exams, free tuition for exemplary students and a Free Trial Class for those who are new to joining in studying together at Neos Famous Music.
